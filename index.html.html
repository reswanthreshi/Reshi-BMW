BMW M4 Competition

The BMW M4 is a high-performance sports coupe from BMW’s M division. It uses a 3.0L twin-turbo inline-6 engine producing up to 503 hp (543 hp in CSL). Available in manual (RWD) or automatic (RWD/AWD) versions. Accelerates 0–100 km/h in 3.4 seconds (Competition xDrive). Features M Adaptive Suspension, Active M Differential, and carbon-fiber roof. Interior includes M Sport seats, digital cockpit, and iDrive 8 infotainment. Supports Apple CarPlay, Android Auto, and Harman Kardon audio. Top speed: 250 km/h (290 km/h with M Driver’s Package). Fuel efficiency: 8–10 km/l, tank capacity around 60 liters. Price in India (2025): ≈ ₹1.53 crore (M4 Competition xDrive).
