About the BMW M4

The BMW M4 is a high-performance coupe powered by a 3.0L TwinPower Turbo inline-6 engine that produces up to 503 horsepower. It delivers exceptional performance with 0–100 km/h acceleration in just 3.4 seconds (Competition xDrive variant). Featuring M Adaptive Suspension, Active M Differential, and lightweight carbon-fiber elements, it offers precision and agility. The interior includes advanced M Sport seats, digital displays, and BMW’s iDrive 8 infotainment system with CarPlay and Android Auto. Its estimated mileage is 8–10 km/l, and it has a 60L fuel tank. Price in India (2025): ₹1.53 crore (approx).
